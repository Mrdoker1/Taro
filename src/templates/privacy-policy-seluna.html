Privacy Policy - Seluna App

Effective Date: October 13, 2025

Overview

Seluna App ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application for tarot readings, affirmations, and spiritual reflection.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address (if you create an account)
  • Tarot Readings: Questions, spread selections, and reading results
  • Affirmations: Custom affirmations and selected topics
  • Journal Entries: Personal notes and reflections in the calendar
  • Preferences: Language settings, zodiac sign, and customization options

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: App features used, crash reports for improving app stability
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage patterns to enhance user experience

How We Use Your Information

  • Tarot Services: To provide personalized tarot readings and interpretations
  • Affirmations: To generate and deliver daily affirmations
  • Spiritual Journal: To save and organize your personal reflections
  • AI Processing: To generate interpretations using AI services
  • App Improvement: To fix bugs and enhance user experience

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your readings and journal entries are primarily stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Sync: Optional cloud backup for your data (if enabled by user)
  • Security: We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data
  • AI Processing: Reading interpretations may be processed through secure third-party AI services
Important: We respect your privacy and spiritual journey. Your personal readings and reflections never leave your device unless you explicitly enable cloud backup features.

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with:

  • AI Services: For generating tarot interpretations and affirmations
  • Google/Apple Services: For app distribution and basic analytics
  • Cloud Storage: For optional data backup (only if enabled)

Your Rights

  • Access: View your stored readings and data
  • Deletion: Delete your readings, journal entries, and account data at any time
  • Opt-out: Disable optional features like cloud sync and analytics
  • Data Export: Export your spiritual journal and readings

To request account deletion, please use our Account Deletion Request Form.

Data Retention

  • Tarot readings and affirmations are kept until you delete them
  • Journal entries are stored locally on your device
  • Usage analytics are anonymized after 12 months
  • Account data is deleted immediately upon request

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

AI and Content Generation

Seluna App uses artificial intelligence to:

  • Generate tarot card interpretations based on traditional meanings
  • Create personalized affirmations
  • Provide spiritual guidance and insights

All AI-generated content is for entertainment and reflection purposes only.

International Users

Your data may be processed in countries where our service providers operate, including the United States and European Union.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page.
