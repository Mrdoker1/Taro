Privacy Policy - Smart Doc Scanner

Effective Date: September 23, 2025

Overview

Smart Doc Scanner ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Documents and Images: Photos and documents you scan using our app
  • Payment Information: When making purchases through our payment processor (Alpha Bank)
  • Account Information: If you create an account (optional)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: App features used, crash reports for improving app stability
  • Camera Access: Only when you actively use the scanner feature

How We Use Your Information

  • Document Processing: To provide OCR and AI-powered document analysis
  • App Functionality: To save, organize, and manage your scanned documents
  • Payment Processing: To handle subscription payments through Alpha Bank
  • App Improvement: To fix bugs and enhance user experience

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your documents are primarily stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Backup: Optional cloud sync (if enabled by user)
  • Security: We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data
  • AI Processing: AI analysis may be processed through secure third-party services
Important: We prioritize your privacy. Your personal documents never leave your device unless you explicitly enable cloud backup features.

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with:

  • Alpha Bank: For payment processing
  • AI Services: For document analysis and OCR
  • Google/Apple Services: For app distribution and basic analytics

Your Rights

  • Access: View your stored data
  • Deletion: Delete your documents and account data
  • Opt-out: Disable optional features like cloud sync
  • Data Export: Export your documents at any time

To request account deletion, please use our Account Deletion Request Form.

Data Retention

  • Documents are kept until you delete them
  • Payment records are retained as required by law
  • Usage analytics are anonymized after 12 months

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Users

Your data may be processed in countries where our service providers operate, including the United States and European Union.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page.
